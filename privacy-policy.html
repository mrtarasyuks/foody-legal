1. Overview

Foody ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered mobile application that helps you manage your fridge inventory, discover recipes, track nutrition, and more. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By creating an account and using Foody, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

This policy applies to Foody for iOS and Android. The app is operated by the Foody development team. Our support email is ecoberleco@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the app.

Account & Profile Data

  • Email address and password (used for authentication)
  • Full name, age, and gender (optional, used to personalise AI suggestions)
  • Profile photo / avatar
  • Dietary type (e.g. vegetarian, vegan), food restrictions, and excluded ingredients
  • Daily calorie goal

Fridge & Inventory Data

  • Product names, categories, quantities, units, storage locations, and expiration dates you enter or that are detected by AI from photos
  • Purchase history and receipt data (product names, prices, categories) from scanned receipts
  • Shopping lists generated by AI or created manually

Nutrition & Health Data

  • Calorie intake logs (source, amount, description, date)
  • Macronutrient data (protein, fat, carbohydrates)
  • Water intake logs

AI Chat & Recipe Data

  • Chat messages you send to the AI Chef assistant
  • Recipes saved or generated within the app
  • Chat session history

Media & Input

  • Photos you take or select from your gallery (fridge photos, receipt photos, food photos)
  • Voice recordings you make for product or calorie recognition

Game & Rewards Data

  • ICE virtual currency balance and transaction history
  • In-app game scores (Foody Catch)

Technical & Usage Data

  • App language preference
  • Theme and display preferences
  • Notification preferences
  • Usage counts for rate-limited features (stored locally on device)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide, improve, and personalise the Foody experience:

  • Authentication & account management: to create and secure your account
  • Personalised AI features: your dietary preferences, fridge contents, and profile data are sent to AI services to generate relevant recipes, shopping lists, dish suggestions, and nutrition estimates
  • Fridge management: to store and display your inventory, expiration dates, and categories
  • Calorie & nutrition tracking: to calculate and display your daily nutrition progress
  • Receipt analysis: to extract product names and prices from photos of receipts using AI
  • Spending analytics: to show you spending summaries and category breakdowns from your receipts
  • Shopping lists: to generate and store AI-produced shopping lists
  • AI Chef chat: to process your messages and fridge contents to produce cooking suggestions and recipes
  • Foodygram (social feed): to publish, display, and moderate food posts (Premium feature)
  • ICE rewards: to track your virtual currency balance and reward history
  • Customer support: to respond to your enquiries

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. AI Processing

Foody uses AI to power its core features. To provide these features, certain data is transmitted to third-party AI providers via our secure server-side proxy (Supabase Edge Functions). Your API keys and credentials are never stored on your device.

The following data may be sent to AI providers for processing:

  • Fridge contents (product names, quantities) — for recipe and dish suggestions
  • Photos (fridge, food, receipts) — for product and receipt recognition, calorie estimation, and Foodygram analysis
  • Voice audio recordings — for product and calorie voice input recognition
  • Text messages — for AI Chef chat and calorie text input
  • Profile data (dietary type, restrictions, calorie goal) — to personalise AI responses
  • Spending category data — for AI spending advice

AI providers used:

  • Google Gemini 2.5 Flash (primary provider) — operated by Google LLC
  • Anthropic Claude Sonnet / Haiku (fallback provider) — operated by Anthropic PBC
Important: Content you submit for AI analysis (photos, voice, text) may be processed and temporarily retained by Google and Anthropic in accordance with their respective privacy policies and data processing terms. Please review Google's Privacy Policy and Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

AI responses are generated automatically and may not always be accurate. You should not rely solely on AI-generated content for food safety, dietary, or medical decisions.

5. Photos & Voice Input

Photos: When you take or upload a photo (fridge, food, or receipt), the image is compressed on-device and transmitted securely to our backend (Supabase Storage) and to our AI proxy for analysis. Fridge photos are stored in a private, access-controlled storage bucket. Foodygram food photos are stored in a public storage bucket (as they are intended to be shared in the social feed).

Voice recordings: When you use the voice input feature, a short audio recording is captured on your device and transmitted in base64-encoded format to our AI proxy for processing. Voice recordings are not permanently stored on our servers; they are processed in real time and discarded.

You can revoke microphone and camera permissions at any time through your device settings. Revoking these permissions will disable the corresponding features in the app.

6. Foodygram Social Feed

Foodygram is an optional Premium feature that allows users to share photos of food dishes with other Foody users.

  • Photos you post on Foodygram are stored in a publicly accessible storage bucket and visible to all Foodygram users
  • Your display name and avatar may be visible alongside your posts
  • AI automatically analyses food photos you submit to Foodygram before posting
  • Other users may report posts; posts with multiple reports may be hidden pending review
  • You can delete your own posts at any time within the app

Please do not post photos that contain identifiable people without their consent, or any content that violates our Terms of Use.

7. Subscriptions & Payments

Foody offers optional paid subscriptions (Foody Premium) and in-app purchases. All payment transactions are processed by the following services:

  • RevenueCat — our in-app purchase and subscription management platform
  • Apple App Store — for purchases made on iOS devices
  • Google Play — for purchases made on Android devices

We do not store your payment card details or billing information. All financial transactions are handled entirely by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase event data and subscription status for the purpose of managing entitlements. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

ICE virtual rewards are an in-app virtual currency with no real-world monetary value and are not subject to payment processing.

8. Data Storage & Security

Your account data, fridge inventory, recipes, chat history, calorie logs, and other app data are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase (supabase.com). Access is protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies, meaning each user can only access their own data.

Authentication credentials (session tokens) are stored securely on your device using the operating system's secure storage mechanism (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) via expo-secure-store.

Daily usage counters (for rate-limited features) and certain display preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and are not transmitted to our servers.

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
